Guidelines for Submitting Feedback:

1. Enter your name in the "Name" field.

2. Enter your Gmail address in the "Email" field.

3. Write your feedback in the "Feedback" field. Please provide as much detail as possible.

4. Click "Submit Feedback" to send your feedback to the Cut Pro team.

5. Complete the reCAPTCHA challenge to verify that you are not a robot.
